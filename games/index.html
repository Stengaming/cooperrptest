game rules

Respect all players – No insults, harassment, or bullying. No RDM (Random Deathmatch) – Do not kill other players without a valid roleplay reason. No VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch) – Do not intentionally run over players with a vehicle. Drive realistically – Follow traffic rules and do not drive recklessly. No exploiting or glitch abuse – Using bugs or hacks will result in a ban. No abusing emergency buttons – Do not make false 911 calls or spam emergency signals. Roleplay is required – Everyone must participate in realistic roleplay. No FailRP – Actions that are unrealistic or impossible in real life are not allowed. No Metagaming – Do not use out-of-character information in roleplay. No Powergaming – Do not force actions on other players without their consent. No Combat Logging – Do not leave the game to avoid an arrest or a fight. Use weapons realistically – Weapons should only be used appropriately in roleplay. No unnecessary police chases – Police must have a valid reason to start a pursuit. No Cop Baiting – Players should not provoke police officers without roleplay justification. No Mass RDM or Mass VDM – Killing multiple players randomly is a serious offense. No AFK farming – Do not stay in-game just to earn money without playing. No advertising – Promoting other servers or services is forbidden. No NSFW or inappropriate content – Sexual, racist, or offensive content is not allowed. Follow admin instructions – Administrators have the final say in disputes. Use common sense – If something clearly breaks the roleplay spirit, do not do it.
